Welcome

Located in the heart of New Hampshire's White Mountains, LoughKey Hotel is one of America's truly grand hotels. We combine first-class comfort and casual elegance with attentive, personalized service. Our classic The LoughKey Hotel presents a dazzling array of offerings and amenities all wrapped in a timeless storybook setting.

A Winter Fairy-tale at LoughKey

The LoughKey Hotel and Mountain Resort is home to excellent ski slopes, a varied range of dishes and a rich selection of accompanying activities. Experience an unforgettable winter fairy-tale!

Accommodation

Enjoy the beautiful natural environment that we are carefully protecting just for you here at the LoughKey Hotel and Mountain Resort! Treat yourself to the comfort of the four-star LoughKey Hotel and experience the joy of this alpine paradise in a lakeside apartment.

ATMOSPHERE IS EVERYTHING.

Hold your next meeting in a luxurious and unforgettable setting high in the LoughKey Hotel . The minute you arrive and take in the cool mountain air, beautifully landscaped grounds, and awe-inspiring view, the enchantment has already begun. With 20,000 square feet of flexible meeting and event space—including indoor and outdoor locales—you'll enjoy an impressive selection of venue options designed to help you create your ideal meeting or event.

MEETING VENUES

We offer elegant and historical meeting and event rooms easily transformed into exactly what you need to host a magical gala, high-profile board meeting, successful conference, corporate retreat or intimate gathering. Whether you are having a large conference or a small group gathering, we have a wide range of locations to suit your tastes.

MEETING OFFERS

Happy days in many ways

LoughKey Hotel is a destination unto itself, we are very close to some wonderful attractions sure to offer fantastic fun and diversions for the whole family. You will delight in the eclectic array of attractions, state parks, and quaint New Hampshire towns situated right around the corner from our resort.
